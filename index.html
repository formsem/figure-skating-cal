About Combo Scoring
• BV of all jumps in the combo are added together
• GOE is applied from the most difficult jump in the combo
• Euler (1Eu) used as a connecting jump, BV = 0.00
• Element in 2nd half of program → BV × 1.1
⚠️ Program structure rules (max elements, which jumps are allowed per program type/level) are defined in ISU Special Regulations & Technical Panel Handbook. Always verify with official ISU documents for competition use.
